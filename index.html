About Our Weirdness

BIZARRE THREADS was born from a fever dream of our designer, who woke up one night with visions of clothing that defied the laws of physics and conventional aesthetics.

We believe fashion should disturb, confuse, and ultimately transform the wearer and observer alike. Our pieces are not just garments; they are catalysts for perceptual shifts.

Each collection explores the boundaries between the comfortable and uncomfortable, the beautiful and the unsettling, the real and the surreal.

Our materials are sourced from both conventional and unexpected places. We've been known to use everything from technical fabrics that change color with body temperature to experimental textiles grown in laboratories.
